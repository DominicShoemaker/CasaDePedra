House Rules

Welcome to our home! We’ve worked hard to make this a comfortable space. To ensure everyone has a great stay and we stay on good terms with our neighbors, please follow these rules:

  • Check-in & Check-out: Check-in is anytime after 3:00 PM. Check-out is before 11:00 AM. This allows our cleaning team enough time to prepare for the next guest.
  • Occupancy: Only up to 10 guests total are allowed on the property at any time. No unauthorized visitors or overnight guests.
  • No Parties or Events: We have a strict "no party" policy. Excessive noise or large gatherings will result in immediate cancellation of your stay without a refund.
  • Quiet Hours: Please be mindful of our neighbors between 10:00 PM and 8:00 AM.
  • Smoking & Vaping: This is a 100% smoke-free and vape-free property. Smoking is prohibited indoors and near windows.
  • Pets: No pets allowed.
  • Care of Property: Please treat our home as you would your own. If something breaks, please let us know immediately so we can fix it for the next guest.
  • Trash & Recycling: Please place all trash in the designated bins.
