Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 19, 2026

  1. Information We Collect: When you book a stay, we collect personal information including your name, email address, phone number, and payment details. We may also request a copy of a government-issued ID to verify your identity.
  2. How We Use Your Information: We use your data solely for the following purposes:
    • Processing your reservation and payment.
    • Communicating check-in instructions and property updates.
    • Complying with local tax or law enforcement regulations.
    • Improving our guest experience.
  3. Data Security: Your payment information is processed through secure, encrypted third-party gateways (Stripe). We do not store your full credit card details on our local servers.
  4. Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell your data. We only share information with third parties necessary to fulfill your stay (e.g., payment processors or local tourism authorities if required by law).
  5. Your Rights: You may request to view, edit, or delete your personal information at any time by contacting us directly at support@casadepedra.rio.
