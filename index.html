House Rules

Welcome to our home! We’ve worked hard to make this a comfortable space. To ensure everyone has a great stay and we stay on good terms with our neighbors, please follow these rules:

  • Check-in & Check-out: Check-in is anytime after 3:00 PM. Check-out is before 11:00 AM. This allows our cleaning team enough time to prepare for the next guest.
  • Occupancy: Only up to 10 guests total are allowed on the property at any time. No unauthorized visitors or overnight guests.
  • No Parties or Events: We have a strict "no party" policy. Excessive noise or large gatherings will result in immediate cancellation of your stay without a refund.
  • Quiet Hours: Please be mindful of our neighbors between 10:00 PM and 8:00 AM.
  • Smoking & Vaping: This is a 100% smoke-free and vape-free property. Smoking is prohibited indoors and near windows.
  • Pets: No pets allowed.
  • Care of Property: Please treat our home as you would your own. If something breaks, please let us know immediately so we can fix it for the next guest.
  • Trash & Recycling: Please place all trash in the designated bins.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 19, 2026

  1. Information We Collect: When you book a stay, we collect personal information including your name, email address, phone number, and payment details. We may also request a copy of a government-issued ID to verify your identity.
  2. How We Use Your Information: We use your data solely for the following purposes:
    • Processing your reservation and payment.
    • Communicating check-in instructions and property updates.
    • Complying with local tax or law enforcement regulations.
    • Improving our guest experience.
  3. Data Security: Your payment information is processed through secure, encrypted third-party gateways (Stripe). We do not store your full credit card details on our local servers.
  4. Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell your data. We only share information with third parties necessary to fulfill your stay (e.g., payment processors or local tourism authorities if required by law).
  5. Your Rights: You may request to view, edit, or delete your personal information at any time by contacting us directly at support@casadepedra.rio.
